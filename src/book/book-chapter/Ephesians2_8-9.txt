Ephesians 2:8-9

Verse: "For it is by grace you have been saved, through faith—and
this is not from yourselves, it is the gift of God—not by works,
so that no one can boast."

Explanation: This verse tells us that it emphasizes the concept of grace
in salvation, highlighting that it is a gift from God rather than something
earned through human effort. It underscores the importance of faith.
